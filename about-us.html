About Us

Welcome here - Who are we?

In case you don't know, Study Smart is a new learning and revision platform generally based on students who are doing their IGCSEs right now but can also apply to the checkpoint students as well.

What we provide...

Study Smart is a New and free learning platform which has serveral resources. Study Smart is full of sub-tools to push you on to Study more in less time. To mension a few we have true/false quizes, slip and slide and much more!

Slip and Slide is like a small online power point reader that helps you view some of the content we post online - it is very simple and straight-forward to use. You can also enable full screen for a better view but to move the slides in this mode you have to use the right and left arrows.

This is a simple user-friendly quiz sub-tool that Study Smart provides that enables you to test yourself on various topics. These are best for lessons where you have a test comming up! Each of these quizes consist of 10 questions and then results will be displayed at the end of each quiz. On the top right side it diplays how many points out of 10 you have gained so far.
